Scrambled eggs

Here is a recipe for scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan, over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. One the butter has melted, add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as though you are making an omelette.)
  5. when the eggs are still moist, remove the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate, until it is served)
