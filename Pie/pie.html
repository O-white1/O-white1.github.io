Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe

One 9 inch pie

30 minute prep time

40 minute total

8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup; white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all; purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp; cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp; salt
  • 1 1/2 cups; water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp; butter
  • 4 egg yolks; beaten
  • 1 9nch pie crust; baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp; white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven:

    2. preheat oven to 350*F

  2. Make Lemon filling:

    3. In a medium sauce pan:

    • Mix together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium heat, stiring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place eggs yolks in a bowl, and gradually mix in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • bring to a boil, continue cooking while stirring until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into backed pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue:

    4. in a large glass or metal bowl.

    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread Meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake

    5. Bake in preheating oven for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
